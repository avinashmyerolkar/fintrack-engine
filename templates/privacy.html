Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account and use the Service, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment card details, government identifiers, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate your account and display your personal expense history.
  • Generate spending summaries and category breakdowns within your dashboard.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service.

We will never use your financial data to serve advertisements or to build profiles for third-party marketing.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon confirmation, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load the typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms that track you across the web, or any service that receives your expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided on this site.

For information on your broader legal rights under applicable data protection law, please refer to your local data protection authority.
